Farmers | Buyers Commodity | eMarket

eMarket is web application platform which links Local farmers with potential Buyers of farm products. It is essential as Farmers will be able to advertise their commodities easily. Farmers can have account where they can add all their various agricultural products. These products will be available for display to various buyers from different geographical areas. For ages now, local farmers have been able to produce lots of commodities.

The issue has always lied in unavailability or inadequate buyers as ADMARC cannot buy from All farmers. The coming in of this site will eliminate various issues of transportation as farmers’ will Need to transport upon agreement that a buyer wants the product delivered.
